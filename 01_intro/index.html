<br> #### Week 1: Final Project Preposal Here is an example of a guitar pedal: ![image of guitar pedal](./GuitarPed.jpeg) A guitar pedal is something that takes the input of a guitar and augments the electrical signal to create different effects. This guitar pedal creates a distortion noise which is done by using a circuit that looks something like this. <div class="smaller-image-1"> ![distortion circuit](./DistortionCircut.png) </div> My final project will be to make a digital vs. analog pedal. In the music world, there is a stigma that digital effects are always worse than analog ones. I don’t think this is true. This pedal would have digital and analog effects built into it and a switch to change between the two. This would allow me to test whether digital or analog is “better”.
